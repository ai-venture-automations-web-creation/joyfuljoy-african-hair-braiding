Our Story

Joy in Every Braid,
Culture in Every Strand

At Joyfuljoy, we believe your hair is your crown — and every crown deserves to be worn with pride, confidence, and pure joy.

Nestled in the heart of East Orange, New Jersey, Joyfuljoy African Hair Braiding Salon was born from a deep love for African hair artistry and community connection. Our skilled braiders bring years of expertise, cultural pride, and genuine warmth to every appointment.

We don't just braid hair — we celebrate who you are. Whether you're stepping in for the first time or you've been with us for years, you'll always leave feeling beautiful, seen, and truly cared for.
